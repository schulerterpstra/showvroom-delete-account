# Showvroom Account & Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your Showvroom account and associated data, email:

**schulerterpstra@gmail.com**  
Subject: **Delete My Showvroom Account**

Include:
- Your Showvroom username
- The email on your account

- Account profile
- Garage posts and uploaded images
- Comments and likes
- Follower/following relationships

## Timeline
Deletion requests are processed within **30 days**.
